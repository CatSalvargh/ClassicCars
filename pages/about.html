Our Story

Got a minute?
Let us tell you where we come from:

Founded on the open roads of possibility, H&C Car Rental was born from a desire to turn everyday travel into extraordinary adventures. It all started when our founder, Emma Rivers, couldn’t find a car rental service that met her needs for her dream wedding getaway in the mountains. What she envisioned was more than just a car – she wanted an experience. Something that combined style, function, and a dash of the unexpected. Inspired by this gap in the market, Emma gathered a team of passionate road-trippers, event planners, and automotive experts to create a service designed for life’s big moments – weddings, adventure travel, camping, off-road excursions, and more. The vision was simple: rentals that elevate the journey itself.

From our first fleet of custom SUVs and sleek convertibles, we’ve grown into a brand that’s trusted for delivering not just vehicles, but unforgettable memories. Our service is tailored to help customers step out of the ordinary and into the extraordinary. Whether it’s a romantic ride for a wedding, a rugged 4x4 for mountain trails, or a fully-equipped camper van for a weekend of exploration, H&C provides the perfect companion for every milestone.

Our story continues to evolve with each customer who shares their adventure, making us more than just a car rental company – we are a curator of life’s best moments. And as we expand our fleet and our reach, our mission remains the same: to make every trip feel like the beginning of a great story.

Our mission

We are moved by one main goal: to empower people to embrace life’s adventures, big and small. We believe that every journey, whether it’s to a wedding, a camping site, or down a rugged trail, deserves to be more than just a drive – it deserves to be an experience.
We provide a diverse fleet of vehicles that match the moment: luxury sedans and convertibles for weddings and special occasions; fully equipped camper vans for weekend getaways; rugged 4x4s designed to conquer rough terrain; and stylish SUVs for road trips with friends and family.

Our commitment goes beyond transportation – we are dedicated to creating moments of excitement, comfort, and reliability. Every vehicle in our fleet is meticulously maintained and equipped to enhance the travel experience. With flexible rental options and personalized service, we ensure that your journey starts on the right foot.

We also prioritize sustainability and innovation, investing in eco-friendly vehicles and advanced technologies to reduce our carbon footprint. As we look to the future, our mission remains rooted in providing not just a ride, but a gateway to adventure, connection, and celebration.

At H&C, we don’t just rent cars – we help you create the story you’ll tell for years to come.

Our Vision

We don't want to stop at just renting our cars, we look to become the leading car rental brand synonymous with extraordinary experiences. We aim to redefine what it means to rent a car by transforming each customer’s journey into a memorable adventure.

We see a world where people don’t just rent a vehicle for convenience but for inspiration – a sleek convertible to make a grand entrance at a wedding, a sturdy 4x4 to venture into the unknown, or a cozy camper van to turn a weekend into a lifelong memory.

We envision H&C as a trusted partner for every life event, from the everyday to the exceptional. Our goal is to expand our fleet with innovative and sustainable options, including electric and hybrid models, while enhancing our customer experience through seamless booking processes and tailored packages.

Our long-term ambition is to foster a global community of adventurers who share stories of their H&C. By combining quality, innovation, and heart, we’ll create a brand that people turn to not just for transportation, but for the inspiration to explore, celebrate, and connect.

In this vision, every road is an opportunity, every vehicle a catalyst, and every customer a partner in the adventure. H&C will drive dreams into reality.
